Understanding Rosacea: Causes, Symptoms, and Treatment Options

By Dr. Kashish Kalra in Dermatology
May 16 , 2025   |   8 min read

Rosacea is a common skin condition that is often mistaken for acne or skin sensitivity, which delays diagnosis, allowing symptoms such as redness, visible blood vessels, and flare-ups to worsen. As the symptoms and associated triggers differ from person to person, identifying what causes flare-ups can be challenging. That said, learning more about the condition can help you recognise the triggers and manage the symptoms effectively. This article covers everything on rosacea including the treatments that can help manage its symptoms. Let's start with the basics.Rosacea is a chronic skin condition that primarily affects the face. It typically develops in cycles of flare-ups and remissions, meaning symptoms may come and go over time.. Rosacea is more common in fair-skinned individuals and often worsens with triggers such as sun exposure, stress, spicy foods, or alcohol. Though there is no cure, proper treatment and lifestyle adjustments can help manage symptoms effectively.
