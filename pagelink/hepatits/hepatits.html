Hepatitis Screening: Key Benefits, Prevention & Early Detection

By Dr. Manik Sharma in Gastroenterology, Hepatology & Endoscopy
May 19, 2025   |   5 min read

Planning a family? Ensure a healthy future for your child with thalassaemia screening before marriage and pregnancy. Thalassaemia is a genetic blood disorder that affects the production of hemoglobin, leading to anemia and other health complications. Screening for thalassaemia is crucial for couples planning to marry or conceive, as it helps identify carriers of the disease and assess the risk of passing it on to their children.Early detection through screening can guide couples in making informed decisions about family planning, including options like prenatal testing and genetic counseling. By prioritizing thalassaemia screening, couples can take proactive steps to ensure the health and well-being of their future children.

In conclusion, thalassaemia screening is a vital step for couples considering marriage and pregnancy. It not only helps in understanding the genetic risks involved but also empowers couples to make informed choices for a healthier future.
